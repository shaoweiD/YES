Selecting a Printing Position

  1. After opening the DImatix Drop Manager, click on the Fiducial Camera (highlighted in the picture below.)
  2. Now make sure the Reference point is deselected (the box circled in red should be deselected.)
    • To change the position of the printing design, you can click anywhere on the platen box, noted by no. 2 in the picture above, or change the x-position and the y-position of your controls coordinates.
  3. Once you chose your desired position click on Set Print Origin, noted by no.3 in the picture above.

You have now selected your new printing position.
